The United States of America, commonly referred to as the United States (U.S.) or America, is a constitutional federal republic composed of 50 states, a federal district, five major self-governing territories, and various possessions. The U.S. territories are scattered about the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea. Nine time zones are covered. The geography, climate and wildlife of the country are extremely diverse.

Every US part is unique and beautiful in its own way. I live in Pittsburgh and try to explore as many places as I can, and every new place I visit impresses me with something special.

Interesting fact about US: According to the World Giving Index, Americans are the most likely people in the world to help a stranger.
