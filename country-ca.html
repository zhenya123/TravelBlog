Canada is a country in the northern half of North America. Its ten provinces and three territories extend from the Atlantic to the Pacific and northward into the Arctic Ocean, making it the world's second-largest country by total area and the fourth-largest country by land area. Canada is sparsely populated, the majority of its land territory being dominated by forest and tundra and the Rocky Mountains.

We went to Toronto to visit my uncle. They live in the suburbs but we still had a chance to explore the city. One of the best parts of the trip was going up CN Tower and enjoying the view of Toronto's Downtown. This city is very diverse and breathtakingly beautiful.

Interesting fact about Canada: Canada was named through a misunderstanding. When Jaques Cartier, a French explorer, came to the new world, he met with local Natives who invited them to their ‘kanata’ (‘village’). The party mistakenly thought the name of the country was “Kanata” or Canada.
